## 2. Continuing Your Engineering Journey
* **Contribute to Open Source**: Find issues in libraries like LangGraph or AutoGen. Writing robust mocked tests is a stellar way to start.
* **Join the Community**: Participate in local Agentic AI developer meetups, hackathons, and enterprise engineering forums.
* **Build Hardened Systems First**: Always prioritize system reliability (spans, breakers, AST interpretation, and safety gates) over raw LLM prompt complexity.

Thank you for your dedication to building production-ready, safe, and observable Agentic AI systems!

# Agentic AI Curated Reading List

A comprehensive collection of research papers, industrial publications, and frameworks to support your progression from Zero to Production.

---

## 1. Groundbreaking Research Papers

### Multi-Agent Systems & Coordination
* **"CAMEL: Communicative Agents for 'Mind' Exploration of Large Scale Language Model Society"** (Li et al., 2023)
  * *Focus*: Pioneered cooperative role-playing frameworks.
* **"AutoGPT: An Autonomous GPT-4 Experiment"** (Significant Gravitas, 2023)
  * *Focus*: Early paradigm of chaining thoughts and command execution.
* **"MetaGPT: Meta Programming for Multi-Agent Collaborative Framework"** (Hong et al., 2023)
  * *Focus*: Integrates SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) into agent workflows.

### Reflection & Memory Loops
* **"Reflexion: Language Agents with Verbal Reinforcement Learning"** (Shinn et al., 2023)
  * *Focus*: Self-reflective loops utilizing past trial failures to refine future actions.
* **"Generative Agents: Interactive Simulacra of Human Behavior"** (Park et al., 2023)
  * *Focus*: Complex associative memory structures, reflection, and day-planning.

---

## 2. Safety, Guardrails & Security
* **"Jailbroken: How Does LLM Safety Training Fail?"** (Wei et al., 2023)
  * *Focus*: Underpins the mechanisms of prompt injections and defense architectures.
* **"AST-Based Code Generation Hardening"** (OWASP Cheat Sheet Series)
  * *Focus*: Sandbox best practices, secure container runtimes, and parsing security.

---

## 3. Recommended Technical Books
* **"Designing Data-Intensive Applications"** by Martin Kleppmann
  * *Why*: Crucial for SRE patterns, distributed system states, and resilience controls.
* **"Enterprise Integration Patterns"** by Gregor Hohpe and Bobby Woolf
  * *Why*: The ultimate playbook for routing, messaging, and multi-agent message busses.

---

## 4. Leading Open-Source Ecosystems
* **LangGraph** (LangChain team): Standard for complex cyclic graphs and state persistence.
* **AutoGen** (Microsoft Research): Heavy multi-agent communication and task solving.
* **CrewAI**: Highly opinionated role-based multi-agent coordination system.

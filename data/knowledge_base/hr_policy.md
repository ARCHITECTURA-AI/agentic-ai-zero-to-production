# HR Policy: Work From Home & Remote Work Guidance

## 1. Remote Work Eligibility
All software engineering, product, and design personnel are eligible to work remotely up to 3 days per week. In-office collaboration days are set by individual team leads.

## 2. Equipment Allowance
Eligible employees receive a $500 home-office equipment stipend upon onboarding. This covers monitor screens, keyboards, desks, and ergonomic chairs.

## 3. Core Working Hours
All staff must remain reachable on slack and email during core business collaboration hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST.

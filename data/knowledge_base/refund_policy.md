# Refund Policy: Corporate Accounts & Individual Subscriptions

## 1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
Individual subscription plans are eligible for a 100% full refund if requested within 30 days of the original purchase timestamp. No refund requests are processed after 30 days.

## 2. Professional & Enterprise Teams
Enterprise software license keys are non-refundable after activation. Unused licenses can be refunded within 14 days of acquisition subject to a 10% re-stocking transaction charge.

## 3. Approval Gates & Escalation
Any refund amount exceeding $100.00 USD must go through a Human-in-the-Loop billing manager checkpoint before approval and transaction execution.
